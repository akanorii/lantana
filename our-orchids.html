Orchids by Lantana's

Sayed, the late patriarch of the family, was fascinated by orchids, and was a prolific collector throughout his life. The family continues that tradition to this day, searching the world for the most rare and exquisite specimens that they can find.

Our orchid room is a state of the art facility which is designed to be both beautiful and functional. Precise controls of temperature and humidity ensure the long survival of our orchids. Orchids, if cared for properly, can live for decades! Do you have questions about your orchids? Make an appointment with one of our in-house experts today for a consultation. They can diagnose issues, and provide a plan for long-term health.
