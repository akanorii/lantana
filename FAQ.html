Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you source your flowers from?

We have relationships with farmers all over the Midwest. Whenever possible, we source from right here in Minnesota.

How long will my order take to be prepared?

The turnaround time for each order depends on the size of the order and the time of year. When you place your order we'll give you an estimated delivery or pickup window.

Is it possible to call and change my order after the order has been placed?

Unfortunately, once your order has been placed no changes can be made.

Do you charge a delivery fee?

Yes, our delivery fee is $10 for all orders within 5 miles of our shop and $20 within 20 miles. Please call us for delivery fees on deliveries outside of the Twin Cities metro.
